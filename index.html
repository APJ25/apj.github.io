About Me

I'm Adarsh Pulimoottil Jolly, a dedicated Power Electronics Engineer with a passion for developing energy-efficient systems and innovative converter topologies. My expertise lies in DC-DC converters, inverter designs, and embedded control systems.

With a strong foundation in both theoretical knowledge and practical application, I specialize in magnetic component optimization, power flow management, and advanced control strategies. My work focuses on creating sustainable and efficient power electronic solutions for modern applications.

I'm constantly exploring new technologies and methodologies to push the boundaries of power electronics engineering, with particular interest in renewable energy systems and electric vehicle applications.
