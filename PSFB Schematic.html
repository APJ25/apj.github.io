PSFB Converter Schematic

Detailed circuit diagram for the reconfigurable Phase-Shift Full Bridge converter.

Schematic Overview

The schematic illustrates the key components of the reconfigurable Phase-Shift Full Bridge (PSFB) converter. This includes the full H-bridge inverter stage on the primary side, the high-frequency isolation transformer with its multiple windings, and the output rectifier stage. The design incorporates gate drive circuitry for the MOSFETs and the necessary connections for reconfiguring the primary windings between series and parallel modes of operation.
